My Journey at 42Porto

A learning path to expertise in programming. Cheers to new beginnings...

Since October, 2023

During the beginning of 2023, following a period where I felt that I was no longer developing my skills set, I decided to focus on learning programming.

For that, I enrolled at 42Porto, went through all online selection process stages until I could enroll in the Piscine of August 2023. The Piscine is the last stage of the selection process, and it is a 26-days challenge to learn the basics of programming and for our profile to be evaluated to see if it matches the requirements to be able to enroll in the course.

I was able to go through and on the first days of October I was starting the Common Core at 42Porto. On the first 4 ranks, where I am still at, the course focuses in C language. In later stages, it will focus on full-stack development using various frameworks.

As I was gaining interest on the course, I came to the decision of quitting my job as a mechanical engineer at Visblue to focus solely on the course

Mechanical Engineer at VisBlue

The expectable step forward

2019 - 2023

VisBlue is a spin-off company from the Universities of Porto and Aarhus and it is based in Aarhus, Denmark that develops Vanadium Redox Flow Batteries.

I worked from Portugal to continue the development of Flow Battery solutions started during the period I was a researcher at the University of Porto.

As a usual start-up company, VisBlue was an incredible experience that broaden my knowledge, while giving me a higher scope of action than I would expect from a consolidated company. I developed my work as a mechanical designer in design for production and production documentation but also had the opportunity to work in project submissions. And in those projects, in stack and battery assembly, in the battery management systems and in electrical design for flow battery models based on other chemistries. I was also in close contact and helped in project management.

I lead yearly review meetings with the full company to present the portuguese company results and developments, allowing any element of the team to make questions about all development work done throughout the year.

I took an important role in the development of the technology of the company and lead many communications and meetings with suppliers and partners.

2017 - 2019
